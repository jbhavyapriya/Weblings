What is a Program?

Participate in challenges, workshops & events hosted by Corporates & Government Departments for the chance to win cash prizes, incubation & funding. The MCA Startup Development program supports the development of technology-based startup companies during a program designed to help existing startups improve the viability of their product and market strategy.
Generally entrepreneurs were seen as having the following needs:
• Personal development e.g. confidence building.
• Professional development.
• Solving specific business challenges and issues.
