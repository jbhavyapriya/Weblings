Innovation, i.e. developing applications for the economy from scientific findings, is one of three core tasks that the *********** has set itself, together with research and teaching. In order to meet the global challenges facing humanity, our groundbreaking research contributions in the fields of energy, mobility and information should also result in concrete developments and products that contribute to general prosperity and benefit.
Thanks to many good partnerships with business and industry, we succeed in doing this extraordinarily often. With us, the path from the research result to the product is shorter! It is part of the spirit of the **** community that we particularly promote the entrepreneurial spirit that burns within you.




Innovation is at the heart of entrepreneurship. All aspiring business owners should understand what innovation is, why it’s important, and how they can take advantage of it in their own businesses. Howard Schultz, chairman and chief executive of Starbucks for over 20 years, describes innovation: “Innovation must be disruptive. And by disruptive, I mean disruptive. You gotta fracture and break the rules and disrupt.” Our platform helps the new startups and individuals with groundbreaking proposals get the right strategies and resources needed for innovation and supports the development of the company.
